APPLICANT QUALIFICATIONS

  1. Must be aged 15-25 years old on the time of application
    • teens aged 12-14 years old may still apply to the organization but for exposure purposes only e.g. being able to join, discuss, and contribute to committee conversations
    • individuals aged 26 and above may apply for pro bono consultative roles for the youth who need it.
  2. Must be based in the Province of Bohol on the time of application.

Note: Membership decisions will not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, sex, gender identity, and sexual orientation.
